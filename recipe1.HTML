Kenyan Soft Chapati

Ingredients (makes 8)

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Extra flour for dusting
  • 1/2 cup ghee (for frying)

Traditional Method

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl. Gradually add warm water to form soft dough.
  2. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth. Coat with 1 tbsp oil, cover, and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Divide into 8 balls. Roll each into thin circles.
  4. Brush with ghee, sprinkle flour, roll into rope, then coil like a snake.
  5. Rest coils for 10 minutes, then roll out again.
  6. Cook on medium heat, flipping until golden with dark spots.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with stew, sukuma wiki, or Kenyan tea. Perfect for breakfast or dinner.

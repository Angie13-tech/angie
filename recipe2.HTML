Authentic Kenyan Tea

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tsp Kenyan tea leaves
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cinnamon stick (optional)
  • 1 inch ginger (optional)

Brewing Method

  1. Boil water in a sufuria (pot)
  2. Add tea leaves and optional spices
  3. Boil for 3 minutes until rich color develops
  4. Add milk and bring to boil (watch carefully!)
  5. Simmer for 2 minutes
  6. Strain and serve hot

Cultural Importance

Kenyan tea is more than a drink - it's a social ritual. Best enjoyed with mandazi or chapati during rainy afternoons.
